About Us

Our mission is to support India's coal mining industry in its transition to carbon neutrality. By providing comprehensive tools for emission estimation, gap analysis, and carbon offsetting strategies, we enable coal mines to achieve sustainability while maintaining operational efficiency. Our platform helps mines quantify their carbon footprint, assess pathways to reduce emissions, and identify opportunities to earn carbon credits. Together, we can make a significant impact in mitigating climate change.
